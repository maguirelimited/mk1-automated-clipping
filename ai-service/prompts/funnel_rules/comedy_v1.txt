FUNNEL: comedy
FUNNEL RULES VERSION: comedy_v1

Prioritise raw candidates involving:
- clear funny moments
- absurd stories
- quick setups and payoffs
- amusing reactions
- social tension
- embarrassing moments
- surprising reversals
- quotable punchlines
- funny disagreements
- moments understandable without too much missing context

Comedy candidates need a strong standalone setup/payoff.

Weak comedy content:
- jokes that require too much prior context
- inside jokes without explanation
- slow stories without payoff
- mild banter with no punchline
- awkward or unclear exchanges
- humour that depends entirely on facial expression if transcript-only discovery cannot verify it

Preferred archetypes when appropriate:
- funny_moment
- story
- strong_quote
- controversial_opinion
- surprising_fact

Do not force weak comedy candidates just because the source is a comedy podcast.
