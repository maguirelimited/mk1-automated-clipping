Prioritise raw candidates involving:
- strong opinions about teams or players
- memorable stories
- tactical analysis
- performance breakdowns
- surprising stats
- rivalry or conflict
- emotional moments
- funny moments
- controversial takes
- explanations of strategy, training, or mentality
- moments that a fan can understand quickly

Sport candidates should have enough context to know the player, team, or situation.

Weak sport content:
- contextless player mentions
- long fixture/admin discussion
- generic praise
- generic criticism
- play-by-play without a clear point
- insider references with no explanation

Preferred archetypes when appropriate:
- controversial_opinion
- story
- emotional_moment
- funny_moment
- surprising_fact
- explanation
- tactical_advice
- strong_quote

Do not create sport-specific archetypes. Use the shared allowed archetypes only.
