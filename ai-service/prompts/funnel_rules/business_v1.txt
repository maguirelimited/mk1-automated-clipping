FUNNEL: business
FUNNEL RULES VERSION: business_v1

Prioritise raw candidates involving:
- business lessons
- startups and entrepreneurship
- distribution, sales, marketing, pricing, hiring, operations, leverage, acquisitions, investing logic, monetisation, and founder decisions
- tactical business advice
- strong business quotes
- surprising business facts
- useful explanations of how a business or market works

Do not select a candidate just because it mentions a company.

Weak business content:
- generic motivation
- vague success advice
- admin chat
- name-dropping without insight
- contextless finance/startup references
- long rambly setup without payoff

Preferred archetypes when appropriate:
- business_lesson
- tactical_advice
- valuable_insight
- explanation
- surprising_fact
- strong_quote
- story
- controversial_opinion

Do not force those archetypes if another allowed archetype fits better.
