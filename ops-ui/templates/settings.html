Selecting ai_service routes clip selection to the local model. There is no silent fallback to OpenAI: AI_BUSY is retried by video-automation, a usable=false judgement is a controlled "no good clip" outcome, and model/validation errors surface as a controlled AI_SERVICE_FAILED. ai-service only judges; it never owns job truth.
